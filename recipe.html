Delicious Chocolate Cake

The perfect treat for every occasion!

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 cups of flour
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 1 tsp of baking powder
  • 1 tsp of vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350¬∞F (175¬∞C).
  2. In a bowl, combine flour, sugar, cocoa powder, and baking powder.
  3. Whisk in eggs, milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  4. Pour the batter into a greased cake pan and bake for 30 minutes.
  5. Let the cake cool, then frost with your favorite chocolate frosting!

Notes

If you want a richer cake, try adding some chocolate chips into the batter. You can also top the cake with fresh berries for a delicious twist!
