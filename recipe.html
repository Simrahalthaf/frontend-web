Welcome to my food blog! Today i am sharing my favourite CHOCOLATE CAKE recipe!

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 and 1/2 cups of flour
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 1 tsp of baking powder
  • 1 tsp of vanilla extract

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175 C)
  2. In a bowl combine flour, sugar cocoa powder, and baking powder.
  3. Whisk in eggs, milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract until smooth
  4. Pour the batter into a greased cake pan and bake for 30 minutes
  5. Let the cake cool, then frost with your favonte chocolate frosting!

    6. Now your delicious cake is ready!
